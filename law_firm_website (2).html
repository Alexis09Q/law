Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge for legal services?
Our fees vary depending on the type of case and complexity. We offer free consultations for most practice areas, and personal injury cases are handled on a contingency basis (no fees unless we win). We provide transparent pricing and payment plans when appropriate.
What should I bring to my first meeting?
Bring any relevant documents related to your case, including contracts, police reports, medical records, correspondence, and identification. The more information you provide, the better we can assess your situation and provide accurate advice.
How long will my case take?
Case duration varies significantly based on complexity, court schedules, and whether the case settles or goes to trial. We'll provide realistic timelines during your consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.
Do you handle cases outside of Miami?
Yes, we represent clients throughout South Florida and can handle cases in multiple counties. For cases outside our primary service area, we'll discuss the specifics during your consultation.
